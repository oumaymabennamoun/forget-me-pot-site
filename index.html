How Does It Work?


Water is stored at a reservoir in the bottom of the pot.

Sensors continuously check the condition and environment of the plant.

Sensor data is sent to the ESP32 microcontroller, the brain of the system.

The microcontroller stays in a low power sleep mode when it is not checking the plant to conserve energy.

Built-in pumps are activated when soil is detected as too dry.

Water is released through small nozzles, evenly across the top of the soil.


Why Choose Forget Me Pot?

Eliminates root rot from wicking systems.

Tailors plant care according to plant species, rather than using a preset water amount that leads to under- or over-watering.

Allows hands-off plant care for a month due to power conserving property.

Evenly waters plants through the tube and nozzle system instead of drowning the roots.

FAQ

How do I set up Forget Me Pot after purchase?

Simply snap a photo of your plant using the companion app! The app identifies the plant species and delivers personalized care instructions (watering, temperature, humidity, and light) from trusted plant databases.

Do I need to check up on the plant often?

Not at all. Forget Me Pot continuously monitors plant conditions and the surrounding environment for you! It notifies you when action, such as refilling the water reservoir or moving the plant to more suitable light or temperature, is needed.
You may select your notification preferences (e.g. phone messages) in the app.

When do I refill the water reservoir?

The reservoir supplies your plant with water for at least 1 month, so you don't have to worry about frequent refills!

How is the pot powered?

Forget Me Pot avoids messy cables by operating on rechargeable batteries. Thanks to the microcontroller's power-saving sleep mode, the pot will last several months before the batteries need to be recharged.

Does it need Wi-Fi to function?

No Internet connection is required for the pot to keep running. The microcontroller's BlueTooth and smart memory features ensure the system remains reliable at all times.
