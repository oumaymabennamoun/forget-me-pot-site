How Does It Work?


The pot includes built-in soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and light sensors.

An ESP32 microcontroller continuously processes sensor data and serves as the brain of the system.

Water pumps are automatically activated by the circuit when dry soil is detected.

Reservoir water is evenly distributed throughout the soil via integrated tubing and nozzles along the top edge.

The ESP32 remains in low-power sleep mode between plant checks to conserve energy.

Users are notified when the plant requires adjustments to light or temperature.

FAQ

How do I set up Forget Me Pot after purchase?

Simply snap a photo of your plant using the companion app! The app identifies the plant species and delivers personalized care instructions from trusted plant databases.
You may then select your notification preferences (e.g. phone messages) on the app.

How often do I refill the water reservoir?

The reservoir supplies your plant with water for 1 month, so you don't have to worry about frequent refills!

How is the pot powered?

Forget Me Pot avoids messy cables by operating on rechargeable batteries. By activating the water pumps only when needed and using the ESP32's power-saving mode, the pot can run for several months before the batteries need a recharge.

Does it need Wi-Fi to function?

No Internet connection is required for the pot to keep running. All watering logic runs locally on the ESP32.
